Your PC and Phone. Connected.

FileBridge is a modern desktop application that instantly turns any folder on your computer into a local file-sharing server. Scan a QR code to view, upload, and delete files from your phone—no cables needed.

Download Latest Release View on GitHub

QR Code Convenience

No more typing IP addresses. Just select a folder, scan the generated QR code with your mobile device, and instantly access your files via a clean web interface.

Two-Way File Management

Don't just view files. FileBridge allows you to effortlessly upload items from your phone to your PC, or delete unwanted files right from your browser.

Internet Accessible

Need to share out of your local network? FileBridge integrates seamlessly with Localtunnel to securely expose your folder via a public URL.
